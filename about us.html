About Us


Bali Safest Driver are experienced and English-speaking tour Bali driver and car located in Kuta just 5 minutes drive from International Ngurah Rai airport, Denpasar-Bali. We aim to provide the best and friendliest service while on vacation. We would like to say thank you so much for your kind attention & trust to open our website. It’s our pleasure to introduce our service for those who live in or outside Bali and want to have fun and exciting Bali sightseeing tour, Bali private tour or other Bali activities. .

We want to contribute to make your vacation here in Bali extra special by using our drivers’ service. We are proud to offer you many kind Bali Safest Driver enjoyable trips, such as Bali Full Day Tour, Bali Activities Tour, Bali Car Hire, Bali Sightseeing Tour. So much to do, so much to see in Bali. Hopefully, our website may become your reference if you have plan to Bali.

A little introduction to Panji (Pronounced Pan-Gee) who is the owner of Bali Safest Driver. Panji is married to Mini and has two wonderful boys Putu and Made. The family live in a small house near Kerobokan which is to the west of Denpasar. It is his ambition to work hard enough with his family and friends who make up the Bali Safest Driver team, to own his own home for his family. He also enjoys his bike riding which hopefully keeps him fit enough to chase after his boys.

Panji loves to have a laugh with people. He is reliable and prompt by responding to emails within the day. He speaks Italian and some German learned from working on cruise ships.
